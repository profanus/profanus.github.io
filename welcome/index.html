Welcome to Profanus

So you've decided to join our amazing guild! We're happy to have you.

We want all our members to feel welcome. Here are some next steps we need to take to help you get settled.

Take a look at our Code of Conduct

Profanus is proud of its community, and a big part of that is our Code of Conduct.

We expect all our members to read and follow these guidelines.

Get an invite to our Slack

We require all members to sign up and join our Slack team. We use Slack to communicate outside of the game. We post important information and announcements here.

You can talk to Oomii to get invited here.

Get a permanent invite to our Discord

We use Discord as our voice-communication tool. There are usually a few people online at any given moment.

You can talk to any of our officers to get invited here.

Install addons for raids

We use some addons during raids that we require all our raiders to install. If you do not have these installed before a raid, we may not invite you or you might not get loot. Note that you just need to install these, you do not necessarily have to configure them or use them outside of raids.

  • Deadly Boss Mods or BigWigs
  • RCLootCouncil
  • Exorsus Raid Tools
  • Handy Notes
